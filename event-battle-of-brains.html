What is BOB?

Brain Battle is a time-bound innovation challenge where participants are given a problem statement on the spot. It tests your ability to think on your feet, work under pressure, and deliver practical solutions.

Teams must understand the problem clearly, identify the root cause, develop a practical solution, and present their idea in a PPT format.

Process & Timeline

1. Analyze

Real-World Problem Statement Provided. Break down and analyze the core issue immediately.

2. Solve (3 Hours)

Build a practical and implementable solution. Teams get 3 hours to analyze and prepare their solution in the provided PPT template.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a working prototype?

No! This is about ideas, not execution. A solid pitch deck is enough.

Can I pitch a business plan?

Yes! Business ideas, social impact ideas, and tech innovations all welcome.

Do investors provide funding?

Winners get mentorship and potential investor connections, not direct funding.

Is registration free?

Yes, completely free! All meals and materials provided.
