What is BoB?

Battle of Brains is a time-bound problem-solving challenge designed to test analytical thinking, creativity, and decision-making skills. Participants will be given a real-world problem statement on the spot and must carefully analyze it, identify the root cause, and develop a practical and realistic solution within the given time. Teams will structure their ideas using the provided PPT template and present their solution before the judges. The event focuses not just on generating ideas, but on clarity of thought, feasibility of implementation, teamwork, and confident presentation under pressure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a working prototype?

No! This is about ideas, not execution. A solid pitch deck is enough.

Can I pitch a business plan?

Yes! Business ideas, social impact ideas, and tech innovations all welcome.

Do investors provide funding?

Winners get mentorship and potential investor connections, not direct funding.
