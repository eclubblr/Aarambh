Innovation In House

All Innovations @ One Place

GITAM Bengaluru Campus
Team of 2-4
Register Now

Overview

Innovation Inhouse is an event where students present their projects and innovative ideas. Students with any innovative idea, prototype, research work, or product can participate and showcase their work. They will receive feedback from faculty and industry experts. The event encourages practical learning and helps students turn their ideas into real-world solutions.

Note: Participants must bring fully prepared projects or posters/publications. No time will be provided during the event to build or develop projects posters/publications on the spot.

Objectives

  • ● To provide a platform for students to present their academic projects or posters/publications.
  • ● To encourage real-world problem-solving.
  • ● To promote interdisciplinary collaboration.
  • ● To recognize and reward outstanding projects or posters/publications.

Who Can Participate ?

Minor Projects

2nd and 3rd year students can present their minor projects or posters.

Major Projects

Final Year Students Can Showcase Major Capstone Projects or Posters.

First Year (TEPE)

First Year (TEPE) Students Can Present Technical Exploration & Product Engineering Projects.

Open Category

Any student from any department can showcase innovative projects or posters.

Faculty Research

Students Can Present Faculty Publications, Patents, or Research Work.

Faculty Innovation Zone

A Dedicated Zone Where Faculty Can Showcase Their Innovations.

Rules & Regulations

Team Composition

2-4 members per team. Only registered members can present.

Logistics

Bring fully charged laptops. Bring own extension boards/components.

Authorized Entry Only

Except Team Members, Others Are Not Allowed Inside During Evaluation.

Display

A3 Posters/Charts allowed.

Report 30 mins early. ID Cards mandatory.
