Overview

Leader – The Origin is a high impact solo leadership simulation where decisions define outcomes. Participants are presented with dynamic, unforeseen scenarios that demand clarity, composure, and conviction. Designed to test clarity, composure, and conviction under pressure, this event calls for disciplined thinking and accountable decision making, requiring participants to interpret complex situations, exercise decisive judgment, and justify their choices with depth, structure, and authority.

Eligibility Criteria

Rules & Regulations

Topic Allotment

Leadership scenarios assigned one week prior. Topics are final. Strict adherence required.

Time Policy

Digital timer displayed. Must conclude within 6 minutes.

No hate speech. Maintain institutional decorum.

The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding.
