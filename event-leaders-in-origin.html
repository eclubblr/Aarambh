Overview

This event is a solo leadership simulation designed to challenge participants beyond theoretical knowledge and into real-world application. Each participant will be assigned a complex leadership scenario one week prior to the event. These scenarios are crafted to test critical thinking, creativity, ethical reasoning, and structured decision making under pressure.
