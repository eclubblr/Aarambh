What is Idea Pitching?

The Idea Pitching Event is a platform designed to encourage students to present innovative ideas, startup concepts, and problem-solving solutions in a structured and competitive environment. The event aims to promote entrepreneurship, creativity, and critical thinking among students across all departments.

Participants will pitch their ideas individually or in teams before a panel of judges within a fixed time limit, followed by a brief Q&A session. The focus will be on originality, feasibility, scalability, and real-world impact of the ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can beginners participate?

Yes! This event is for all skill levels. We provide mentors to help beginners.

Is there a registration fee?

No, it's completely free! We cover food, WiFi, and all materials.

Can I participate solo?

Solo participants can join existing teams or form new teams at the event.

What's the judging criteria?

Innovation (30%), Technical execution (30%), Impact (20%), Presentation (20%)
