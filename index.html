About AARAMBH

AARAMBH is the flagship entrepreneurship and innovation event organised by the E-Club of GITAM Bengaluru. It is a celebration of startups, technology, design, and creative problem solving, dedicated to students, faculty, founders, and innovators who want to build, pitch, and collaborate.

The event includes workshops, pitch competitions, speaker sessions, and hands-on problem-solving activities that foster entrepreneurship and real-world product thinking.
